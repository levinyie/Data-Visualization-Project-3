Movies Data Visualization

Count of Movies per Year

This histogram is of the movies produced over the years based on the data from IMDB. It's increased over the years with technology and according to the data, most movies were produced in 2021-2023. This may be because production was slowed around 2019-2020 when Covid-19 was introduced to the world. Based on the data, we could interpret that the spike in 2022 was because of delays in production for 2020, which catapulted the numbers for 2022. 2023 and 2024 seem to be going back on track, which is a steady increase of movies throughout the years.

Average Runtime of Movies Per Year

This line chart shows the average runtime of movies per year. Based on the chart, we can see that movies between 1950 and 1960 had the longest average runtime, reaching a peak of 210 minutes a movie on average. That's 3 hours and 30 minutes per movie on average. This dramatically dropped as the years go by, resulting in movies maintaining an average runtime of about 110-130 minutes.
